Welcome to My Family

My lovely puppy, welcome to my family.

Aug 02, 2024
2 minute read

After I left IBM, the first thing I wanted to do was get my life back. So I decided to get a puppy. I started looking on different websites, and eventually I found one on 乖乖狗寵物家.

When I first saw her, I felt that she was incredibly adorable, and she had a very special fur color. I knew it was her at that moment. I immediately sent a message to the shop and made a reservation to visit her. I wanted to take her home as soon as possible.

That day, I really looked forward to seeing her. When it was time, I took an Uber, feeling very happy. After about 30 minutes, I arrived at the shop. As soon as I went in, the owner put her on the ground. She was full of energy and kept following me everywhere, even chewing on my pants. It seemed like she knew I was there to take her home. I knew she was the best encounter of my life. Then I started handling the paperwork. However, I didn’t have any dog supplies at home. Fortunately, the shop also sold some basic pet supplies, so I bought them there right away.

When we got home, I opened the box and let her see her new home. Then I started setting up her space. After that, I put her in her place, and I was very surprised because she seemed to know this was her home. She immediately wanted to explore everywhere in the house.

She fit in very well in her new home. After playing for a while, she fell asleep comfortably in her place. The place was temporary, and I planned to prepare a better one for her.

In the end, I took her home, which was a lifelong promise I made to her. I was truly grateful that she became part of my life. From that moment on, we began our journey together.
